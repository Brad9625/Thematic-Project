Game Rules

Objective: Detectives must catch Dr. X. Dr. X must evade capture until the final move.

  1. Roles: 1 player is Dr. X, the rest are Detectives.
  2. Turns: Players take turns moving to a connected location.
  3. Capture: If a Detective lands on Dr. X’s location, Dr. X is caught.
