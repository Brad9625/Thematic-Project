Scotland Yard Official Rules

Here is a brief summary of the rules.

  1. Objectives:
    - Detectives win by locating and capturing Mr X.
    - Mr X wins by evading capture until the final round or if detectives cannot move.
  2. Players and Roles:
    - 2-6 Players.
    - 1 player is secretely chosen to be Mr. X.
    - Others are Detectives
  3. Game Components:
    - Game board (map of London).
    - Player token, Transport tickets: Taxi, Bus, Underground
    - Secret and Double Move tickets (Mr X only)
    - Mr X movement log (hidden record)
  4. Setup:
    - All players have random starting locations.
    - Mr X's location is hidden from detectives.
    - Each player transport tickets.
    - Mr X receives additional Secret and Double Move tickets.
  5. Turn Order:
    - Mr X moves first, followed by detectives in clockwise order
  6. Movement Rules:
    - Players move along connected routes using tickets
    -- Taxi (short routes)
    -- Bus (medium routes)
    -- Underground (long routes)
    - Detectives cannot share locations.
  7. Mr X's Special Rules:
    - Mr X records every move secretly
    - His location is revealed on specfic rounds.
    - Use Secret Tickets to hide transport type
    - Double Move Tickets to move twice in one turn
  8. Capture Rules:
    - If a detective lands on Mr X, Mr X is captured and the game ends.
  9. Winning The Game:
    - Detectives win by capturing Mr X.
    - Mr X wins by surviving all rounds or if detectives cannot move.
  10. Key Restrictions:
    - Detectives cannot use Secret or Double Move tickets.
    - Used tickets go to Mr X.
    - Mr X cannot move onto a detective's space .

!!Good Look and may the best Player win!!
